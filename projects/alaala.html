Instructions:

Click "next" to cycle through words, then attempt to recite the Tagalog translation out loud. Click "translate" to review if you were correct.

Your Papa, Mama, Tito, Tita, Lolo or Lola can help you with pronunciation!

“Retrieval practice—recalling facts or concepts or events from memory—is a more effective learning strategy than review by rereading."
― Peter C. Brown, Make It Stick: The Science of Successful Learning

This tool was created for English speakers wanting to practice their Tagalog. It includes around 4000+ of the most common words in the English language.
We target nouns, adjectives & verbs since you can structure "Taglish" sentences and understand context more easily with these. Kaya mo yan!

powered by Cloud Translation API (Google)

"Alaala" means memory, recollection, remembrance
